Research Participating Companies (For Job Seekers):

Attending a job fair is all about making connections. Before the event, take some time to research the companies that will be participating. Visit their websites, familiarize yourself with their products or services, and identify roles that align with your skills and interests. This will allow you to ask insightful questions during your interactions and demonstrate genuine interest in their work.

Prepare Your Resume and Portfolio (For Job Seekers):

Ensure your resume is updated with your most recent skills and experience. Tailor it to highlight the qualifications relevant to the roles you're interested in. Have multiple copies printed on high-quality paper to distribute to recruiters at the event. If you're a developer, designer, or creative professional, consider bringing a digital portfolio showcasing your best work.

Craft Your Elevator Pitch (For Everyone):

An elevator pitch is a concise and compelling summary of yourself and your value proposition. It should be no longer than 30 seconds and effectively communicate your skills, experience, and career goals. Whether you're a job seeker, entrepreneur, or simply attending to network, having a strong elevator pitch will allow you to make a lasting impression on anyone you meet.

Dress for Success (For Everyone):

First impressions matter! Dress professionally to showcase your confidence and seriousness about the event. Opt for business casual attire or formal wear depending on the specific instructions provided by Route Academy. Feeling polished will boost your confidence and make a positive impact during interactions.

Plan Your Schedule and Set Goals (For Everyone):

Familiarize yourself with the schedule of events, workshops, or talks offered at the Route Tech Summit/Job Fair. Prioritize the sessions that align with your interests and career goals. Set realistic goals for the event, whether it's connecting with a specific number of companies, attending certain talks, or landing an interview. Having a plan will maximize your time and ensure a productive experience.
